Welcome! Tao is an incoming Computer Science Ph.D. Student at Columbia University. He studies human-AI interaction design with Dr. Lydia Chilton.

Supporting users' online behaviors and interactions, Tao's works falls into the field of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Through a human-centered lens, Tao is interested in understanding how to design and build AI-powered technical and social supports for collaborative environments and marginalized communities.

During Tao's undergrad at Cornell University, he conducted research with Dr. Andrea Won, Dr. Natalie Bazarova, Dr. Susan Fussell, and Dr. François Guimbretière. He also worked as a teaching assistant for Dr. Gilly Leshed and Dr. Kyle Harms.
